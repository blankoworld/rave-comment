Rave Comment, a commenting
system that uses Isso

An alternative to get comments on your webblog

Rave Comment is a service that provides you a way to display and manage comments on your webblog by using email.
It's compatible with any weblog engine that permit to change templates, especially static weblog engines.

The only thing you need is to subscribe to the free service. After this you will receive an email that will explain you how to proceed.
